## 📸 Exemplos de Prompts por Categoria

### **1. NUTRIÇÃO - Versão Premium**

```
PROFESSIONAL EDITORIAL PET PHOTOGRAPHY - PREMIUM QUALITY

SUBJECT: adorable golden retriever puppy with expressive brown eyes happily eating 
premium pet food from a modern stainless steel elevated bowl in a bright, airy 
Brazilian home kitchen with large windows and tropical plants visible.

SCENE DETAILS: The adorable golden retriever puppy with expressive brown eyes has 
perfectly groomed, soft and shiny fur catching the natural light. Captured mid-meal 
with an endearing, content expression showing genuine enjoyment

LIGHTING: Golden hour sunlight streaming through sheer white curtains creates a warm, 
inviting glow with soft shadows. Professional three-point lighting setup with natural 
fill light

BACKGROUND & ENVIRONMENT: Clean, minimalist modern kitchen with white marble 
countertops, light wood cabinets, and subtle green plants. Background softly blurred 
using f/2.8 aperture

COMPOSITION & CAMERA: Shot at pet eye-level using 50mm prime lens. Rule of thirds 
applied with pet positioned at intersection point. Shallow depth of field isolates 
subject beautifully

MOOD & ATMOSPHERE: Warm, nurturing, and wholesome - evoking feelings of home and care

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS: Shot with professional DSLR, 50mm f/1.8 lens, ISO 400, 
1/250s shutter speed

STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- Photorealistic, magazine-quality editorial photography
- Pinterest-optimized vertical composition (2:3 ratio, 1000x1500px)
- Professional color grading with natural, vibrant tones
- Authentic Brazilian context and aesthetic
- Sharp focus on subject with beautiful bokeh background
- Genuine emotion and connection captured
- No artificial elements, CGI, or illustrations
- No text overlays, watermarks, or graphic elements
- Suitable for veterinary marketing, pet care blogs, and Pinterest

PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE: Editorial pet photography, lifestyle documentation, professional 
veterinary marketing content, Brazilian pet care industry standard.
```

## 🔧 Implementação Técnica

### **Sistema de Templates:**
Cada categoria agora possui 9 elementos:
1. `animal` - Descrição detalhada do animal
2. `scene` - Ação/situação específica
3. `setting` - Local e contexto
4. `details` - Detalhes visuais ricos
5. `lighting` - Iluminação profissional
6. `background` - Ambiente detalhado
7. `composition` - Técnica fotográfica
8. `mood` - Atmosfera emocional
9. `technical` - Especificações de câmera

### **Construção Dinâmica:**
```python
# Adapta baseado no título
animal = template["animal"]
scene = template["scene"].format(animal=animal)
setting = template["setting"]
# ... todos os elementos

# Monta prompt estruturado
prompt = f"""PROFESSIONAL EDITORIAL PET PHOTOGRAPHY - PREMIUM QUALITY

SUBJECT: {animal} {scene} {setting}.
SCENE DETAILS: {details}
LIGHTING: {lighting}
...
"""
```
