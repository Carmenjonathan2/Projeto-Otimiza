## 🍖 CATEGORIA: NUTRIÇÃO

### **Exemplo 1: Alimentação Natural para Cães**

```
PROFESSIONAL EDITORIAL PET PHOTOGRAPHY - PREMIUM QUALITY

SUBJECT: adorable golden retriever puppy with expressive brown eyes happily 
eating premium pet food from a modern stainless steel elevated bowl in a bright, 
airy Brazilian home kitchen with large windows and tropical plants visible.

SCENE DETAILS: The adorable golden retriever puppy with expressive brown eyes 
has perfectly groomed, soft and shiny fur catching the natural light. Captured 
mid-meal with an endearing, content expression showing genuine enjoyment

LIGHTING: Golden hour sunlight streaming through sheer white curtains creates 
a warm, inviting glow with soft shadows. Professional three-point lighting 
setup with natural fill light

BACKGROUND & ENVIRONMENT: Clean, minimalist modern kitchen with white marble 
countertops, light wood cabinets, and subtle green plants. Background softly 
blurred using f/2.8 aperture

COMPOSITION & CAMERA: Shot at pet eye-level using 50mm prime lens. Rule of 
thirds applied with pet positioned at intersection point. Shallow depth of 
field isolates subject beautifully

MOOD & ATMOSPHERE: Warm, nurturing, and wholesome - evoking feelings of home 
and care

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS: Shot with professional DSLR, 50mm f/1.8 lens, 
ISO 400, 1/250s shutter speed

STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- Photorealistic, magazine-quality editorial photography
- Pinterest-optimized vertical composition (2:3 ratio, 1000x1500px)
- Professional color grading with natural, vibrant tones
- Authentic Brazilian context and aesthetic
- Sharp focus on subject with beautiful bokeh background
- Genuine emotion and connection captured
- No artificial elements, CGI, or illustrations
- No text overlays, watermarks, or graphic elements
- Suitable for veterinary marketing, pet care blogs, and Pinterest

PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE: Editorial pet photography, lifestyle documentation, 
professional veterinary marketing content, Brazilian pet care industry standard.
```
