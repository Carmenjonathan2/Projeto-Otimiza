### **3. Técnicas Fotográficas Profissionais**

✅ **Especificações Técnicas:**
- **Lentes:** 50mm f/1.8, 85mm f/1.4, 100mm f/2.8, 35mm f/1.4
- **ISO:** 400-1600 (baseado na cena)
- **Shutter Speed:** 1/125s - 1/500s
- **Abertura:** f/1.4 - f/2.8 (bokeh profissional)
- **Iluminação:** Golden hour, three-point, soft box
- **Composição:** Regra dos terços, ângulos específicos

### **4. Estrutura em 9 Elementos**

Cada prompt agora possui:

1. **SUBJECT** - Animal + ação + local brasileiro
2. **SCENE DETAILS** - Descrição visual rica
3. **LIGHTING** - Técnica de iluminação profissional
4. **BACKGROUND & ENVIRONMENT** - Ambiente detalhado
5. **COMPOSITION & CAMERA** - Especificações técnicas
6. **MOOD & ATMOSPHERE** - Emoção evocada
7. **TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS** - Configurações de câmera
8. **STYLE REQUIREMENTS** - Requisitos de qualidade
9. **PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE** - Estilo editorial

Professional high-quality photograph of a golden retriever puppy eating

### ✅ **DEPOIS (V2.0 PREMIUM):**

```
PROFESSIONAL EDITORIAL PET PHOTOGRAPHY - PREMIUM QUALITY

SUBJECT: adorable golden retriever puppy with expressive brown eyes 
happily eating premium pet food from a modern stainless steel elevated 
bowl in a bright, airy Brazilian home kitchen with large windows and 
tropical plants visible.

SCENE DETAILS: The adorable golden retriever puppy with expressive 
brown eyes has perfectly groomed, soft and shiny fur catching the 
natural light. Captured mid-meal with an endearing, content expression 
showing genuine enjoyment

LIGHTING: Golden hour sunlight streaming through sheer white curtains 
creates a warm, inviting glow with soft shadows. Professional three-point 
lighting setup with natural fill light

BACKGROUND & ENVIRONMENT: Clean, minimalist modern kitchen with white 
marble countertops, light wood cabinets, and subtle green plants. 
Background softly blurred using f/2.8 aperture

COMPOSITION & CAMERA: Shot at pet eye-level using 50mm prime lens. 
Rule of thirds applied with pet positioned at intersection point. 
Shallow depth of field isolates subject beautifully

MOOD & ATMOSPHERE: Warm, nurturing, and wholesome - evoking feelings 
of home and care

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS: Shot with professional DSLR, 50mm f/1.8 lens, 
ISO 400, 1/250s shutter speed

STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- Photorealistic, magazine-quality editorial photography
- Pinterest-optimized vertical composition (2:3 ratio, 1000x1500px)
- Professional color grading with natural, vibrant tones
- Authentic Brazilian context and aesthetic
- Sharp focus on subject with beautiful bokeh background
- Genuine emotion and connection captured
- No artificial elements, CGI, or illustrations

PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE: Editorial pet photography, lifestyle documentation, 
professional veterinary marketing content, Brazilian pet care industry 
standard.
```

**Melhorias:**
- ✅ Contexto brasileiro específico
- ✅ Especificações técnicas completas
- ✅ Iluminação detalhada (golden hour, three-point)
- ✅ Composição profissional (regra dos terços, 50mm)
- ✅ Emoção e atmosfera definidas
- ✅ 3x mais detalhado
